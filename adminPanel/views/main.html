About Opal

Opal is a multiplatform application that allows patients to obtain information about their treatment and waiting times.
These tool will allow you as a doctor or administrator to obtain all the information that is currently being shown to the patient.
The Opal Admin Panel offers the following features:
  • Obtain a real time view of the information that is currently shared with the patients.
  • Keep an open channel of communication with the patients via chat message.
  • See all the documents that have currently being published to the patients.
  • Update the information that is currently being shown to the patient about you.
